📘 Learn Stock Analysis

SMA: Simple average of past closing prices.

EMA: Like SMA but recent prices matter more.

MACD: Fast EMA - Slow EMA.

Signal Line: EMA of MACD.

📊 Example: Axis Bank’s 10-day SMA = ₹900 if last 10-day prices average ₹900.

MACD crossover: Buy = MACD > Signal. Sell = MACD < Signal.

RSI: Above 70 = overbought. Below 30 = oversold.

📈 Example: ICICI Bank RSI falls below 30 → buy signal?
